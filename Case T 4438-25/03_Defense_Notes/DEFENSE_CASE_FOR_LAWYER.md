# PART 7: LEGAL ARGUMENTS

## 7.1 Burden of Proof

**Swedish Law:** Plaintiff must prove:
1. Money was transferred
2. Transfer was a LOAN (not business/hawala)
3. Repayment was agreed and not made

**Defense Position:**
- Even if transfers proven, PURPOSE was business not loan
- Repayment happened in Damascus
- No meeting of minds for loan agreement

## 7.2 Witness Credibility

**Case Law:** Courts can discount testimony from witnesses with:
