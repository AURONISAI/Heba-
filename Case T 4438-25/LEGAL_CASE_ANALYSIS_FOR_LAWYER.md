## 4.1 Burden of Proof

In Swedish civil cases for debt claims (fordran), the **plaintiff bears the burden of proof** to establish:

1. That a loan (f√∂rstr√§ckning) existed
2. That the defendants received the money
3. That there was an agreement to repay
4. The terms of repayment (amount, due date)

### Standard of Proof
- Civil standard: "more likely than not" (√∂verv√§gande sannolik)
- NOT criminal standard of "beyond reasonable doubt"
