- You will be ordered to pay the full amount claimed

5. Whether you **accept** or **dispute** the claims
6. If disputing - explain WHY
7. Any evidence you want to present
8. List any witnesses (name, address, email, phone)

---

## RESPONSE OPTIONS

### Option 1: Accept (Medge)
- Admit you owe the money
- Court will order payment
- You pay plaintiff's legal costs

### Option 2: Dispute (Bestrida)
- Deny you owe the money (or the amount)
- Must explain your grounds
- Case proceeds to hearing

### Option 3: Partial Dispute
- Accept some claims, dispute others
- Explain which parts and why
